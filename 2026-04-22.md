### 力扣刷题(185，)
#### 部门工资前三高的所有员工
- 子查询，前三高
-     SELECT
      d.name AS Department,
      e.name AS Employee,
      e.Salary
      FROM Employee e
      JOIN Department d ON e.DepartmentId = d.Id
      WHERE
      -- 核心：比当前工资高的不同工资数量小于3 = 前三
      (SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT salary) 
       FROM Employee 
       WHERE DepartmentId = e.DepartmentId 
         AND salary > e.salary) < 3;
